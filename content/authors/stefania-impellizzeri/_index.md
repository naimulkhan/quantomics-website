Stefania Impellizzeri is Associate Professor in the Department of Chemistry and Biology at Toronto Metropolitan University, where she holds the **Jet Ice Research Chair in Sustainable Materials Chemistry**. She is also **TMU's Dimensions Faculty of Science Lead for EDI in Research**. Her research centers on the synthesis and application of nanomaterials — including quantum dots and nanoparticles — for sensing and photonic applications. To date, she has trained **over 40 HQP**.

In QuantOmics, Dr. Impellizzeri contributes to **Stream 1 (Quantum Probe Design & Fabrication)**, guides the synthesis of quantum nanomaterials for biosensor applications, and serves as the program's **EDI lead**. She co-teaches **Course 1.2 — Quantum Nanotechnology for Life Sciences** and leads **Course 1.5 — Responsible Innovation & EDI in Precision Health**.
