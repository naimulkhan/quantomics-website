Shayan Rayan is Assistant Professor at the University of Saskatchewan, with research expertise spanning computational biology, bioinformatics, and the application of quantum nanotechnology to next-generation genomic sequencing platforms. He brings a unique cross-disciplinary perspective bridging Stream 1 (hardware) and Stream 2 (genomics).

In QuantOmics, Dr. Rayan serves as **Institutional Lead at the University of Saskatchewan**, co-leads **Stream 1 (Quantum Probe Design & Fabrication)** alongside Dr. Valente, contributes to genomic signal integration in **Stream 2**, and co-teaches **Course 1.2 — Quantum Nanotechnology for Life Sciences**.
