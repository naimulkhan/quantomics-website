Harry Ruda holds the **Stanley Meek Chair in Advanced Nanotechnology** at the University of Toronto. His research focuses on semiconductor materials for sensing applications, with particular emphasis on photonic sensor technology. He has contributed significantly to the field of photonic sensor technology and has trained **over 20 HQP**.

In QuantOmics, Dr. Ruda guides training in the design and fabrication of photonic components for quantum biosensors, enhancing their sensitivity and specificity as part of **Stream 1 (Quantum Probe Design & Fabrication)**. He also co-teaches **Course 1.1 — Biosensor Engineering for Precision Health**.
