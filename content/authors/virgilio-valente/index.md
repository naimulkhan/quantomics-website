Virgilio Valente is Assistant Professor in the Department of Electrical, Computer, and Biomedical Engineering at Toronto Metropolitan University, where he leads the **Wireless Sensing Lab** (10+ HQP). His research focuses on the design of integrated CMOS/MEMS sensor systems for implantable and injectable wireless biosensor networks. He aims to develop miniaturized, energy-efficient devices for real-time health monitoring and diagnostics. He has trained **15+ graduated HQP**.

In QuantOmics, Dr. Valente co-leads **Stream 1 (Quantum Probe Design & Fabrication)**, guiding trainees through the development of next-generation quantum biosensing platforms, and co-teaches **Course 1.1 — Biosensor Engineering for Precision Health** alongside Dr. Ruda and Dr. Mahshid.
