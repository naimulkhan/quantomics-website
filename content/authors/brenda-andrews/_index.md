Brenda Andrews is Professor in the Department of Molecular Genetics at the University of Toronto, where she holds a **Tier 1 Canada Research Chair in Systems Genetics and Cell Biology**. Her pioneering work in functional genomics and systems biology has significantly advanced our understanding of gene networks, earning her recognition as a global leader in the field. She has mentored **over 100 graduate students and postdoctoral fellows**, many of whom have become leaders in academia and industry.

In QuantOmics, Dr. Andrews contributes her expertise to **Stream 2 (Genomics Signal Integration)**, guiding research projects that integrate genomic data with quantum sensing technologies. She also co-teaches **Course 1.3 — AI in Genomics**.
