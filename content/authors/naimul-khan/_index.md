Naimul Khan is an Associate Professor in the Department of Electrical, Computer, and Biomedical Engineering at Toronto Metropolitan University (TMU), and the Director of the Multimedia Research Laboratory. He has secured over **$3 million** in research funding from NSERC, OCE, MITACS, and SSHRC, and has trained **over 50 highly qualified personnel** whose graduates have advanced to roles in academia and industry worldwide.

His research focuses on multimodal machine learning, integrating AI, imaging, and biosensing technologies for healthcare applications — including physiological signal analysis for arrhythmia, therapeutic virtual reality with emotion recognition, and AI-driven diagnostics from neonatal ultrasound.

As **Program Director of QuantOmics**, Dr. Khan oversees the overall operation of the program, leads **Stream 3 (AI-Powered Therapeutic Design)**, co-teaches the Multimodal-Omics Data Integration Bootcamp (1.4), coordinates interdisciplinary integration across all streams, and co-teaches the AI in Genomics course (1.3).
