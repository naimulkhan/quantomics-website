Brett Trost is Assistant Professor in the Department of Molecular Genetics at the University of Toronto and a Scientist at **The Hospital for Sick Children (SickKids)**. His research involves the development of computational tools for genomic data analysis and multi-omic integration, with a focus on identifying genetic variants associated with human diseases, particularly neurodevelopmental disorders.

In QuantOmics, Dr. Trost serves as **Institutional Lead at the University of Toronto**, co-leads **Stream 2 (Genomics Signal Integration)**, developing pipelines to process and interpret data from quantum biosensors. He also co-teaches the **Multimodal-Omics Data Integration Bootcamp (1.4)** alongside Dr. Khan.
