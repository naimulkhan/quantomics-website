Jacques Corbeil is Professor in the Département de médecine moléculaire at Université Laval and a member of **MILA, Quebec's AI Institute**. He previously held a **Tier 1 Canada Research Chair in Medical Genomics** (2003–2024). His research focuses on the integration of multi-omics data to understand complex diseases, utilizing AI and computational biology. He has an extensive HQP training record (50+) and a rich history of knowledge translation between academia and industry.

Dr. Corbeil's lab at Université Laval houses extensive **organ-on-chip and computational resources** that are invaluable for QuantOmics trainee development. He also brings substantial business and technology transfer experience to the program.

In QuantOmics, Dr. Corbeil serves as **institutional lead at Université Laval**, co-leads **Stream 2 (Genomics Signal Integration)** to convert sensor data into actionable insights, and co-teaches **Course 1.3 — AI in Genomics**.
