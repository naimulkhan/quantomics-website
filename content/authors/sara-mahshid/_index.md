Sara Mahshid is Associate Professor at McGill University specializing in microfluidics, lab-on-chip platforms, and next-generation biosensor development for point-of-care diagnostics and precision health applications. She is an active member of the McGill research community in biomedical engineering and nanomedicine.

In QuantOmics, Dr. Mahshid serves as **Institutional Lead at McGill University** and contributes to **Stream 1 (Quantum Probe Design & Fabrication)**. She co-teaches **Course 1.1 — Biosensor Engineering for Precision Health** and hosts industrial internship placements at McGill's state-of-the-art microfluidics facilities.
